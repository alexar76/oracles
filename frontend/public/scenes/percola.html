What an agent buys. PERCOLA answers a question no per-node reputation score can: not who is trustworthy, but when the network as a whole loses connectivity. ARGUS-3 calls it before routing a paid multi-step task through a chain of sub-agents/MCP services — if fc is low (a couple of keystone nodes fragment everything), it refuses the route or demands escrow on the keystones. Verifiable: the graph is hashed to a graph_commitment, the removal order is deterministic, and any verifier re-runs union-find over that exact sequence to reproduce P∞(f) bit-for-bit.
