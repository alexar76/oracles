What an agent buys. ABLATION answers a question no single-service health check can: not is this node up, but is the whole dependency mesh poised at criticality, where one routine failure detonates a system-wide cascade. ARGUS-3 reads τ and the tail mass before committing a paid, multi-step task to a fan-out of MCP services — a heavy tail (small τ) means a rare giant avalanche is overdue, so it throttles concurrency, stages escrow, and keeps a fallback warm. Verifiable: the grain sequence is seeded and deterministic, each toppling is the abelian sandpile rule, so any verifier replays the exact drops and reproduces every avalanche size — and the fitted τ — bit-for-bit. Criticality is measured, not asserted.
